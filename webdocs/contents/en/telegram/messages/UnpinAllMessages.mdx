## Overview 
Unpin all message in chats.
## Parameters 
```ts 
ctx.telegram.unpinAllMessages(chatId)
```
- `chatId` (_number|string_) 
    - Chat/Groups/Channel id. 
## Example 
```ts:index.ts {2} showLineNumber
bot.command("unpinAll",async (ctx)=>{
    let results = await ctx.telegram.unpinAllMessages(ctx.chat.id)
    console.log(results)
})
```
